Domain 3: Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing

Overview

Welcome to Domain 3: Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing! In this domain, you'll embark on a journey to acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to ethically assess and reinforce digital systems against cyber threats. From grasping the legal and ethical foundations to conducting penetration tests, this domain comprehensively covers essential aspects of ethical hacking and penetration testing methodologies.

Learning Objectives

After completing this domain, the participant will be able to:

  • Understand the legal and ethical frameworks governing ethical hacking practices.
  • Explore various penetration testing methodologies such as OSSTMM and PTES.
  • Learn passive and active information gathering techniques for footprinting and reconnaissance.
  • Familiarize yourself with footprinting tools like Maltego and theHarvester.
  • Master network scanning techniques including port scanning and OS fingerprinting.
  • Learn service enumeration techniques and banner grabbing methods.
  • Familiarize yourself with vulnerability scanning tools like Nmap and Nessus and understand their role in identifying network weaknesses.
  • Learn network hardening techniques to strengthen network security against potential attacks.
  • Understand exploitation frameworks like Metasploit and Exploit-DB.
  • Explore privilege escalation techniques such as kernel exploits and identifying misconfigured services.
  • Gain proficiency in using post-exploitation tools like Meterpreter and Cobalt Strike.

Key Topics Include

  • Legal and Ethical Frameworks
  • Penetration Testing Methodologies (OSSTMM, PTES)
  • Footprinting and Reconnaissance Techniques
  • Passive and Active Information Gathering
  • Footprinting Tools (Maltego, theHarvester)
  • Network Scanning Techniques (Port Scanning, OS Fingerprinting)
  • Service Enumeration and Banner Grabbing
  • Exploitation Frameworks (Metasploit, Exploit-DB)
  • Privilege Escalation Techniques
  • Post-Exploitation Tools (Meterpreter, Cobalt Strike)
